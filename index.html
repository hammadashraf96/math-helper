Scan & Solve

Point your camera at any math problem — handwritten or printed. AI analyzes it and returns step-by-step solutions with difficulty ratings.

📚

Structured Curriculum

Browse lessons from Elementary through Advanced college math — Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Statistics, Trigonometry, and more.

🧩

Practice Quizzes

Test yourself with quizzes by category and difficulty. Track your 7-day streak and monitor improvement over time.

🔖

Save & Revisit

Save any scanned problem to your personal library. See your stats — scans saved, problems solved, and topics covered.
